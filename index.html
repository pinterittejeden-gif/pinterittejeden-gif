working rules

Keep the evidence and the proposal in separate columns.

This four-part check is deliberately repetitive. It makes unsupported jumps easier to spot and gives every project a clear way to fail.

  1. 01

    source material

    The published mechanisms and existing work I can point to directly.

  2. 02

    proposal

    The unproven connection I am making between those pieces.

  3. 03

    failure case

    The biological, ecological, or material result that would stop the project.

  4. 04

    next test

    The smallest useful experiment that would change my mind.
